Refund, Resend, and Returns Policy for PrepPal

Effective Date: [Effective Date]

1. Delayed Orders

In the event that an order experiences significant delays, PrepPal will take the following steps:

  • If an order for the USA is delayed for more than 45 days (counting from the date of payment), or 60 days for other countries (except for certain countries with different shipping methods), PrepPal will investigate the issue.
  • If a customer files a complaint regarding a delayed order, please open a dispute on the CJ APP and provide proof of the customer's complaint or email stating non-receipt.
  • If the tracking information does not show any movement or information, CJDropshipping.com will take appropriate actions.

2. Delivered Orders

If tracking information indicates that an order has been delivered and your customer claims non-receipt, PrepPal will:

  • Require a non-delivery certification issued by the local post office with an official seal.
  • Address any tracking information alerts (incorrect/insufficient address, unknown recipient, etc.) with the respective local distributor.
  • If the order has been delivered and your customer still claims non-receipt, PrepPal will not be held responsible.

3. Products Damaged

In the event that products arrive damaged, PrepPal will:

  • Offer a full refund or replacement for badly damaged products.
  • Offer a partial refund or replacement for products that arrive partially damaged.
  • Request photos or videos of damaged items to prove the damage. If photos do not suffice, please provide a video.

4. Incorrect or Missing Products

In the event that products arrive damaged, PrepPal will:

  • Offer a full refund or replacement for badly damaged products.
  • Offer a partial refund or replacement for products that arrive partially damaged.
  • Request photos or videos of damaged items to prove the damage. If photos do not suffice, please provide a video.

Important Notes

PrepPal can only open disputes while the order status is not closed.

Force Majeure: PrepPal is not responsible for product damage or shipping delays caused by factors beyond its control.

Shipping Method Limits: Some shipping methods are not trackable in certain areas, and PrepPal will not accept disputes related to these cases.

Destination Limits: There are limitations on shipping to specific countries, and PrepPal will not accept disputes for orders shipped to these regions.

Return

Products can be returned to CJ China warehouses, but it's not recommended due to the high international shipping cost and potential damage during the return process.

Unacceptable Disputes

PrepPal will not accept unreasonable disputes, including issues such as buyer preferences, product descriptions, unusual smells, incorrect orders by the buyer, address errors, and more.

This Refund, Resend, and Returns Policy is effective from [Effective Date]. PrepPal and CJDropshipping.com retain the right to modify this policy at any time, with the modified agreement becoming valid upon the signature of both parties.
